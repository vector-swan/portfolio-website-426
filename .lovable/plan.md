

# Mia's Portfolio — Senior Product Designer, AI Agent UX

## Design System
- **Colors**: Electric cobalt blue (`#2563EB` / `#1D4ED8`), vivid coral (`#FF6B35`), clean white, near-black for text
- **Typography**: Google Fonts — **Syne** (bold geometric display headlines) + **Inter** (clean body text). Large, confident sizing.
- **Radius**: Slightly rounded (8px). No heavy shadows — clean and modern.

## Pages & Sections (Single Page, Smooth Scroll)

### 1. Hero Section
- Asymmetric layout with bold oversized headline: *"Designing the AI experiences people will actually trust"*
- Name "Mia" + title "Senior Product Designer" with a coral accent
- CTA button "View My Work" that smooth-scrolls to case studies
- Subtle animated gradient or geometric shape element for visual energy
- Fade-in and slide-up entrance animations

### 2. About Section
- Grid-breaking layout: text on one side, photo placeholder (styled colored block) on the other
- 2–3 punchy sentences about her AI design expertise
- Animated underline on key phrases

### 3. Case Studies Section
- 3 bold project cards with hover scale effects and color accents:
  - "Multi-Surface Agent Design"
  - "GenAI Text Framework"  
  - "Prompt Engineering Studio"
- Each card has a short description, tags, and a colored accent bar
- Cards animate in on scroll (staggered fade-in)

### 4. Skills/Expertise Section
- Visual chip/tag layout: AI Agent UX, Conversational Design, Multi-Surface Design, Design Systems, Responsible AI
- Bold colored chips with hover states
- Asymmetric arrangement, not a boring grid

### 5. Contact Section
- Clean, minimal — headline + email link + social links
- Coral accent CTA button

## Technical Details
- Scroll-triggered animations using Intersection Observer
- Smooth scroll behavior
- Fully mobile responsive with adjusted typography scales
- Syne + Inter loaded via Google Fonts
- Real copy throughout — no lorem ipsum
